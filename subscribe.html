Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about AgroX

What is AgroX?

+

AgroX is an AI-powered agricultural platform that helps farmers make smarter decisions through crop diagnostics, weather forecasting, soil analysis, and market insights. Our platform combines machine learning with real-time data to provide actionable recommendations for better farming outcomes.

How does the crop diagnosis work?

+

Simply upload a photo of your crop using your smartphone. Our AI analyzes the image to identify the crop type, growth stage, health status, and detects any diseases or pests. You'll receive instant recommendations on how to address any issues.

Do I need special equipment to use AgroX?

+

No special equipment is required! You only need a smartphone with a camera and internet connection. For soil monitoring, we offer optional IoT sensors that can be integrated with the platform for real-time data collection.

Is my farm data secure?

+

Yes, security is our top priority. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry-leading security practices and never share your information with third parties. Your data belongs to you and can be exported at any time.

What subscription plans are available?

+

We offer multiple subscription tiers to suit different farm sizes and needs. Plans include Basic, Pro, and Enterprise options with varying features and support levels. Contact us for detailed pricing information tailored to your requirements.

Can I try AgroX before subscribing?

+

Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial that includes access to all core features. No credit card required. Sign up today to start exploring AgroX and see how it can transform your farming operations.

How accurate are the weather forecasts?

+

Our weather predictions use advanced AI models trained on historical data and multiple weather sources. Forecasts are typically 85-90% accurate for 7-day predictions, with higher accuracy for shorter-term forecasts. We continuously improve our models with new data.

What crops does AgroX support?

+

AgroX supports over 50 major crop types including cereals (wheat, rice, corn), vegetables (tomatoes, peppers, lettuce), fruits (citrus, berries), and legumes. We're continuously expanding our database to include more crops.
